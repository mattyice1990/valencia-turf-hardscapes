Questions? We've Got Answers

The stuff everyone asks before they call (and you should too)

How much does artificial turf cost in Tucson?

Real talk: most residential projects run $8-15 per square foot, all-in. That includes removal, base prep, premium materials, installation, infill, and cleanup. No hidden fees. We'll give you a detailed written quote with options so you know exactly what you're getting. Get a free estimate.

Is artificial grass safe for dogs and kids?

Absolutely! Our turf is non-toxic, lead-free, and safer than natural grass (no pesticides, fertilizers, or herbicides ever). Kids love it, dogs love it. Plus, it's soft and cushioned for play.

Does artificial turf smell with pets?

Not if you rinse it regularly! Our pet turf drains 30+ inches per hour (faster than any Tucson monsoon), and it has antimicrobial backing that prevents odor-causing bacteria. Quick weekly rinse keeps it fresh. We've got customers with 3-4 large dogs who love their turf.

How does artificial turf hold up in the Arizona sun?

Our turf is built for this. UV-stabilized fibers won't fade or degrade even in 110°+ heat. We're talking 15-20+ years of perfect green lawn. All our products come with manufacturer fade warranties because we only use desert-rated materials.

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects are done in 1-2 days. Larger or complex projects might take 3-4 days. We'll give you an exact timeline with your quote. Fast, clean, and you can use your new yard immediately after we're done.

What maintenance is required?

Honestly? Almost none. Occasionally rinse it with a hose, brush high-traffic areas to keep fibers upright, and remove debris. That's it. No mowing, no fertilizing, no watering. Ever.
