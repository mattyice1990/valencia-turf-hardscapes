Common Questions About Getting an Estimate

How quickly will I hear back?

We typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours, and always within 24 hours. Same-day consultations are often available! Learn more about our services.

Do you charge for estimates?

No! Estimates and consultations are completely free with no obligation. We provide detailed written quotes at no charge.

What information do you need?

Your contact info, property location, and basic project details. Photos are helpful but not required - we'll measure on-site.

Can I get a phone estimate?

We can provide ballpark ranges over the phone, but accurate quotes require an on-site visit to assess your specific property and needs.

Do you offer weekend appointments?

Yes! Saturday appointments are available from 8am-3pm. We can also accommodate evening consultations on weekdays.

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects are completed in 1-2 days. We'll provide a specific timeline in your quote based on your project size and complexity.
