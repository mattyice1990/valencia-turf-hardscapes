## 🎨 CTA Design Principles

### Visual Hierarchy:
1. **Primary CTAs**: Green background, white text, prominent size
2. **Secondary CTAs**: White background, green text, outlined
3. **Text CTAs**: Green text, underlined, integrated in content

### Accessibility:
- ✅ High contrast colors (8.7:1 ratio)
- ✅ Clear focus indicators
- ✅ Touch-friendly sizes (44px+ on mobile)
- ✅ Descriptive text (not just "Click Here")

### Mobile Optimization:
- ✅ Full-width buttons on small screens
- ✅ Stacked vertically for easy tapping
- ✅ Larger touch targets
- ✅ Sticky CTA adapts to mobile layout

### Conversion Rate Improvements:
