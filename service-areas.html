Professional Artificial Turf Installation Throughout Pima County

Valencia Turf & Hardscapes is proud to serve homeowners and businesses across all of Southern Arizona. With 20+ years of local experience, we understand the unique challenges of each area—from the desert heat of Marana to the mountain views of Catalina Foothills. No matter where you're located in Pima County, we bring the same expert installation, premium materials, and outstanding service to your door.

Our Primary Service Areas in Pima County

Tucson (All Areas)

Serving every neighborhood across Tucson including Central, East, West, South, and Northwest Tucson. From historic downtown to the sprawling suburbs, we've installed artificial turf and hardscapes in every corner of the city.

Oro Valley

Premium installations throughout Oro Valley's master-planned communities. We specialize in putting greens, pet-friendly turf, and complete backyard transformations that complement your mountain views.

Marana

Expert service for Marana's growing communities. From residential lawns to commercial properties, we handle projects of all sizes with the desert expertise Marana homeowners need.

Catalina Foothills

Luxury turf and hardscape installations for Catalina Foothills estates. Tournament-quality putting greens, designer paver patios, and complete landscape transformations.

Sahuarita

Professional installations serving Sahuarita's family-friendly neighborhoods. Pet-friendly turf systems, backyard makeovers, and water-saving solutions for growing families.

Green Valley

Trusted service for Green Valley homeowners. Low-maintenance turf solutions perfect for active adult communities. Putting greens, paver patios, and complete outdoor living spaces.

Vail

Expert installations throughout Vail and the surrounding area. We handle the unique drainage challenges of this region with proper grading and monsoon-ready systems.

Flowing Wells

Residential and commercial turf installation for the Flowing Wells area. Cost-effective solutions that save water and eliminate maintenance.

Tanque Verde

Premium service for Tanque Verde's scenic properties. Custom designs that blend with the natural desert landscape while providing lush green spaces.

Corona de Tucson

Reliable installations for Corona de Tucson residents. We bring professional turf and hardscape solutions to this growing Southeast Tucson community.
