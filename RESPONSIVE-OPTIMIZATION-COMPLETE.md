## 🎯 Google SEO Standards Met

### 1. Minimum Font Sizes
- ✅ **Body text: 16px minimum** on all devices
- ✅ **Paragraphs: 16px** with 1.6 line-height
- ✅ **Form inputs: 16px** (prevents iOS auto-zoom)
- ✅ **Buttons: 16px minimum** text
- ✅ **Navigation links: 16px** on mobile

### 2. Touch Target Sizes
- ✅ **All buttons: 48x48px minimum** (Google standard)
- ✅ **All links: 48px minimum height**
- ✅ **Form inputs: 48px minimum height**
- ✅ **Interactive elements: 48x48px**

### 3. Content Fit & Readability
- ✅ **No horizontal scrolling** at any viewport size
- ✅ **All text wraps properly** with word-break
- ✅ **Images are responsive** (max-width: 100%)
- ✅ **Tables scroll horizontally** when needed
- ✅ **Proper line-height** (1.6 for body, 1.3 for headings)

### 4. Layout Optimization
- ✅ **Flexible grids** that adapt to screen size
- ✅ **Mobile-first responsive** design approach
- ✅ **Proper padding/margins** at all sizes
- ✅ **No content overflow** on any element
- ✅ **Max-widths on containers** prevent extreme stretching

## 🔧 CSS Improvements Made

### Global Optimizations
```css
- overflow-x: hidden on html and body
- word-wrap: break-word on all elements
- max-width: 100% on all content
- Proper box-sizing: border-box
```

### Responsive Breakpoints Added
```css
@media (max-width: 320px)  - iPhone SE 1st gen
@media (max-width: 360px)  - Very small phones
@media (max-width: 480px)  - Small phones
@media (max-width: 600px)  - Intermediate size
@media (max-width: 768px)  - Tablets/phones
@media (max-width: 900px)  - Large phones/small tablets
@media (max-width: 1024px) - Tablets
@media (max-width: 1100px) - Pre-hamburger menu
@media (max-width: 1150px) - Navigation condensing
@media (max-width: 1200px) - CTA button optimization
@media (max-width: 1300px) - Nav spacing optimization
@media (min-width: 1600px)  - Large screens
```

### Typography Scales
- **Desktop H1**: 3.5rem (56px)

### Interactive Elements
